# My Lens Diary

> **A thoughtful, privacy-first contact lens companion for iPhone.**

My Lens Diary helps you keep track of your contact lens wear,
replacement schedule, inventory, and comfort---all while respecting your
privacy. Built exclusively for Apple platforms, it uses modern Apple
technologies to deliver intelligent insights without compromising your
trust.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## ✨ Features

-   👁️ Track left and right lenses independently
-   📦 Manage your contact lens inventory
-   ⏱️ Record insertion and removal times
-   🔔 Receive wear and replacement reminders
-   🌦️ Capture environmental conditions with Apple WeatherKit (optional)
-   🤖 Discover patterns using Apple Intelligence (where available)
-   🎙️ Log events with Siri and App Intents
-   📱 Live Activities for at-a-glance status
-   ☁️ Optional iCloud sync using CloudKit
-   🔒 Privacy-first by design

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 🍎 Built for Apple

My Lens Diary is designed specifically for iPhone and makes thoughtful
use of:

-   Apple Intelligence
-   SwiftData
-   CloudKit
-   WeatherKit
-   Siri
-   App Intents
-   Live Activities

These technologies are used to make the app feel natural, fast, and
secure while keeping you in control of your data.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 🔐 Privacy First

Your data belongs to you.

Wherever practical, your information stays on your device. If you choose
to enable iCloud synchronisation, Apple CloudKit securely syncs your
data across your Apple devices.

My Lens Diary:

-   Does **not** sell your personal information.
-   Does **not** build advertising profiles.
-   Does **not** display third-party advertising.
-   Collects only the information needed to provide the features you
    choose to use.
