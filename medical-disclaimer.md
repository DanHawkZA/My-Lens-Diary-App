Any information, summaries, trends, reminders, statistics, or insights

understand the information that **you choose to record**.

The App does not provide medical opinions or clinical recommendations.

# 2. Not a Substitute for Professional Care

Nothing in the App should be interpreted as medical advice.

Always seek the advice of a qualified optometrist, ophthalmologist, or
other healthcare professional if you:

-   experience eye pain
-   notice sudden changes in vision
-   develop redness or swelling
-   suspect an eye infection
-   have concerns about your contact lenses or eye health

Never ignore professional medical advice because of information
displayed by the App.
