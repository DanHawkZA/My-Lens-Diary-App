# Frequently Asked Questions

## Does My Lens Diary require an account?

No. The app is designed to work without creating an online account.

## Is my information private?

Yes. Your information belongs to you. Wherever possible, data remains on
your device. If you enable iCloud synchronisation, Apple CloudKit
securely synchronises your information across your Apple devices.

## Does the app track my location?

No. The app does not build a location history.

When you choose to log selected events, the app may request your
location to retrieve local environmental conditions using Apple
WeatherKit. This helps identify patterns between your environment and
contact lens comfort.

## Does the app provide medical advice?

No. My Lens Diary is a personal management tool and is not a medical
device.

# Troubleshooting

## Notifications are not appearing

Please verify that:

-   Notifications are enabled in iOS Settings.
-   Focus modes are not suppressing alerts.
-   The reminder is enabled within the app.

## Weather information is unavailable

Check that:

-   Location Services are enabled.
-   My Lens Diary has permission to access your location when needed.
-   An internet connection is available for WeatherKit requests.

## iCloud Sync

If you use iCloud synchronisation:

-   Ensure iCloud Drive is enabled.
-   Confirm you are signed in with your Apple Account.
-   Allow time for CloudKit to synchronise changes.
