# Data & AI Principles

**My Lens Diary -- Contact Lens Manager & Tracker**

**Version:** 1.0\
**Effective Date:** 19 July 2026

## Our Philosophy

Technology should help you understand your habits without compromising
your privacy.

My Lens Diary has been designed around a simple belief:

> **Your data belongs to you. AI should work for you---not on you.**

Our goal is to use modern Apple technologies to provide meaningful
insights while collecting as little information as possible.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Privacy by Design

Privacy is a core design principle, not an afterthought.

Wherever practical:

-   Your information is stored on your device.
-   You decide what information to record.
-   You control whether iCloud synchronisation is enabled.
-   We do not sell your personal information.
-   We do not build advertising profiles.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Why We Use AI

Apple Intelligence may be used to help you:

-   Summarise your contact lens history.
-   Identify recurring comfort patterns.
-   Recognise trends over time.
-   Surface useful observations from your own data.

AI is intended to make your records easier to understand---not to
replace your judgement or your eye care professional.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# AI Is Not Medical Advice

Any AI-generated summaries or insights are informational only.

My Lens Diary does not diagnose medical conditions, recommend
treatments, or provide healthcare advice.

Always consult a qualified eye care professional for concerns about your
vision or eye health.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Environmental Context

If you choose to enable location for selected events, My Lens Diary may
retrieve environmental information using Apple WeatherKit, such as:

-   Temperature
-   Humidity
-   Air quality
-   Weather conditions

This information helps provide context for your own records and may
reveal patterns between your environment and contact lens comfort.

The app is not designed to create a location history or track your
movements.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Apple Technologies

My Lens Diary uses Apple technologies to deliver a secure, integrated
experience, including:

-   SwiftData
-   CloudKit
-   WeatherKit
-   Apple Intelligence
-   Siri
-   App Intents
-   Live Activities

These technologies are used only to provide features that you choose to
use.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Transparency

We believe you should understand how the app works.

If significant changes are made to the way My Lens Diary uses data or
AI, we will update our documentation before those changes are
introduced.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Our Commitment

As My Lens Diary evolves, our guiding principles remain the same:

-   Respect your privacy.
-   Be transparent.
-   Use AI responsibly.
-   Keep you in control of your information.
-   Build features that earn your trust.

Thank you for choosing My Lens Diary.
