# Privacy Policy

**My Lens Diary -- Contact Lens Manager & Tracker**

**Version:** 1.0\
**Effective Date:** 19 July 2026

## Our Privacy Philosophy

My Lens Diary was built on a simple principle:

> **Your contact lens data belongs to you.**

We believe you should be able to manage your contact lens wear without
giving up your privacy.

Wherever possible, your information remains on your Apple device. We
collect only the information necessary to provide the features you
choose to use. We do not build advertising profiles, and we do not sell
your personal information.

# 2. Information You Choose to Record

Depending on how you use the App, you may choose to record:

-   Contact lens prescriptions
-   Lens products and brands
-   Inventory information
-   Lens insertion and removal times
-   Lens wear history
-   Comfort and irritation logs
-   Personal notes
-   App preferences and settings

This information exists solely to provide the features of the App.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 3. Environmental Information

When you choose to log selected events---such as inserting or removing
lenses or recording comfort or irritation---the App may request access
to your location.

Your location is used only to retrieve local environmental conditions
from Apple's WeatherKit service, including information such as:

-   Temperature
-   Humidity
-   Air quality
-   Weather conditions made available by WeatherKit

These environmental conditions may be stored with your log entry to help
you identify patterns between your contact lens comfort and your
surroundings.

My Lens Diary is not designed to track your movements, create a location
history or monitor where you travel.

Location access is optional and can be disabled at any time in iOS
Settings.

-   SwiftData

# 5. Apple Intelligence

Where available, Apple Intelligence may be used to generate summaries,
identify trends and provide helpful insights based on the information
you choose to record.

These features are intended to help you better understand your own data.

They are not intended to diagnose medical conditions or replace
professional medical advice.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 6. Notifications

If enabled, My Lens Diary may send reminders relating to:

-   Lens replacement
-   Wear duration
-   Inventory
-   Other reminders you configure

Notification permissions can be changed at any time through iOS
Settings.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 7. Camera and Photos

If you choose to scan packaging or attach photographs, the App will
request permission to access your camera or photo library.

Images are used only for the features you request.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 8. Advertising

My Lens Diary does not display third-party advertising.

The App does not use advertising identifiers and does not sell your
personal information.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 9. Analytics

At launch, My Lens Diary does not use third-party analytics services or
advertising networks.

Should anonymous analytics be introduced in a future release, this
Privacy Policy will be updated before those features become available.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 10. Data Sharing

My Lens Diary does not sell, rent or share your personal information
with advertisers.

Information is shared only where necessary to provide Apple platform
functionality that you choose to use, such as CloudKit synchronisation,
Siri, Apple Intelligence or WeatherKit.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 11. Data Retention

Information stored locally remains on your device until you delete it,
remove the App or erase your device.

If you enable CloudKit synchronisation, Apple manages synchronised
copies according to Apple's own policies.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

You remain in control of your information.

You may:

-   View your information

-   Delete individual records
-   Delete all information stored by the App
