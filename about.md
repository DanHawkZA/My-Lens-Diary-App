# About My Lens Diary

**Version:** 1.0\
**Effective Date:** 19 July 2026

## Our Story

My Lens Diary was created to solve a simple problem: remembering the
small details that make a big difference when wearing contact lenses.

Many contact lens wearers track replacement dates in a calendar or notes
app, but those tools rarely provide the context needed to understand
long-term habits. My Lens Diary brings your lens inventory, wear
history, comfort logs, reminders, and environmental conditions together
in one place.

Our aim is to help you build better habits and better understand your
own experience with contact lenses.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Built Around Privacy

Privacy is at the heart of My Lens Diary.

Wherever possible, your information stays on your Apple device. When you
choose to enable iCloud synchronisation, Apple CloudKit securely keeps
your data in sync across your Apple devices.

We do not sell your personal information, create advertising profiles,
or collect unnecessary data.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Designed for Apple Platforms

My Lens Diary is designed specifically for Apple's ecosystem and makes
thoughtful use of technologies such as:

-   SwiftData
-   CloudKit
-   WeatherKit
-   Apple Intelligence
-   Siri
-   App Intents
-   Live Activities

These technologies are used to make the app feel natural, helpful, and
deeply integrated with iPhone while respecting your privacy.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# A Personal Journal, Not a Medical Device

My Lens Diary is intended to help you record and understand your own
contact lens habits.

It does not diagnose medical conditions or replace advice from an
optometrist, ophthalmologist, or other qualified healthcare
professional.
